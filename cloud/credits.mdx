## Getting Credits

### Purchase Credits
Buy credits in bundles:
- 50 credits
- 100 credits
- 250 credits
- 500 credits
- Custom amounts available

Credits never expire and can be used across any team.

### Bonus Credits
Earn bonus credits through:
- First-time purchase bonus
- Bulk purchase discounts
- Referral rewards
- Community contributions

## Using Credits

1. Select your team and task type
2. Review credit cost
3. Confirm task details
4. Credits are deducted upon task completion

## Credit Management

### Track Usage
Monitor your credit usage in the dashboard:
- Current balance
- Usage history
- Task breakdowns
- Credit expiration dates

### Share Credits
For organizations:
- Pool credits across teams
- Set usage limits
- Transfer credits
- Generate usage reports

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do credits expire?">
    No, your purchased credits never expire. Use them whenever you need them.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I share credits?">
    Yes, organizations can share credits among team members and set usage permissions.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if a task fails?">
    If a task fails due to system error, your credits are automatically refunded.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Unused credits can be refunded within 30 days of purchase.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
