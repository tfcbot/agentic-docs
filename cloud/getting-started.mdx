Ready to start working with your AI teams? Follow this guide to get up and running quickly with Agentic Team OS Cloud. You'll learn how to set up your account, purchase credits, and begin collaborating with your teams.

## Quick Start Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up for Agentic Team OS Cloud using your work email. You'll get instant access to our platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hire Your Agent">
    Select the agent that matches your current needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Out Intake">
    Complete the intake questionnaire for your selected agent
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Your Deliverables">
    Submit an order to begin processing and your deliverables will be available on the orders page. 
  </Step>
</Steps>
