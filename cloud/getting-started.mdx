Ready to start working with your AI teams? Follow this guide to get up and running quickly with Agentic Team OS Cloud. You'll learn how to set up your account, purchase credits, and begin collaborating with your teams.

## Quick Start Steps

### 1. Create Your Account
Sign up for Agentic Team OS Cloud using your work email. You'll get instant access to our platform.

### 2. Choose Your Team
Select the team that matches your current needs:
- **Market Research Team** for market insights and lead generation
- **YouTube Marketing Team** for content optimization and repurposing
- **Tech Team** for technical documentation and specifications

### 3. Set Up Your Workspace
- Configure your team preferences
- Set up project goals and objectives
- Invite team members (if needed)

### 4. Start Your First Project

## Best Practices

- Start with a clear objective for your team
- Provide detailed context for better results
- Review and provide feedback regularly
- Use the team's specialized capabilities
