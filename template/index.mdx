Build and customize your own AI agent teams with the self-hosted version of Agentic Team OS.
Get full control over your deployment, data, and agent configurations to meet your unique requirements. 

## Why Self-Hosted?

- **Full Control**: Own your data and infrastructure completely
- **Customization**: Extend and modify the platform to meet your unique requirements
- **Learning**: Understand the architecture and components that makes Agentic Team OS work
- **Community**: Join our growing community of developers building on top of Agentic Team OS
