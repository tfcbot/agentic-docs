Agentic SaaS follows the principles of fair-code development and is released under the Sustainable Use License. This license model ensures the project's long-term sustainability while maintaining the benefits of open source for the majority of users.

## What is fair-code?

fair-code is a development model that combines the benefits of open source with the need for sustainable development. It means:

- The source code is publicly available
- You can inspect and modify the code
- You can use the software for any purpose as an individual or non-profit
- Commercial use requires a fair contribution to the project's sustainability

## Our Sustainable Use License

The Sustainable Use License is designed to:
- Protect the rights of individual users and non-profits
- Ensure fair compensation for commercial use
- Maintain transparency and collaboration
- Support long-term project sustainability

## Why This Model?

We believe in:
- **Sustainability**: Ensuring long-term development and maintenance
- **Fairness**: Supporting both open collaboration and fair compensation
- **Innovation**: Encouraging community participation while maintaining quality
- **Transparency**: Clear terms for all users
